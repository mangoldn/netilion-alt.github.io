Designed to let you do more, more simply.

Netilion makes your life easier when working in your plant or any facility made from production lines, measuring devices, pumps, valves, motors or any asset requiring a high level of monitoring and accuracy at any moment of its life time.

Your life will be easier thanks to powerful and live information of every single asset installed in your solution, such as asset health monitoring, documentation mangement, value monitoring and data analytics.

The industry life style has always been in our focus since its begining more than 60 years ago and we carry with us an unlimitely growing experience of the challenges faced by any technician, plant operator or engineer during it’s tough day at work.

Know everything, everywhere, at any time.

Take the information with you! With Netilion you know everything you need to know about your plant or any high level technical installation, everywhere at any time. You gett notified by the important device failures, you will get instant remedy to the failures, you will carry with you all the documentation you need to be as safe and effective as possible.

Your job as a technician, engineer, plant constructor, or plant manager will be supported by 24/7 information on your smartphone or computer, wherever you want and can be shared with whoever you want. We make the information available for you and your colleagues.
